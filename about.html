About Arctic!

Hi I am the main Arctic Dev and I will explain the point of our project. Arctic is a new ipa signer that is made to replace the log filled ESign. Arctic has 2 versions the full release and the beta. The beta is not the stable release so if you can use the full release. Inside of Arctic's Repo is all of Epic Games' games, so you can finally play the games outside of the EU. Arctic is a project that may not become the most popular signer but atleast give it a go.
